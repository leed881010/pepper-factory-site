FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
How do I sync data across devices?
Our apps use iCloud to sync data automatically. Make sure iCloud is enabled for the app in your device settings (Settings > Apple ID > iCloud).
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Select the subscription you want to cancel and follow the instructions.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful!
