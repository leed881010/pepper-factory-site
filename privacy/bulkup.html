'BulkUp' Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Deokhwa Lee ("we," "us," or "our") built the 'BulkUp' app. This SERVICE is provided by Deokhwa Lee and is intended for use as is.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information, as well as your rights regarding your information. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Definitions

  • Service refers to the 'BulkUp' mobile application.
  • Personal Information means information that can identify an individual, either on its own or when combined with other information.
  • Data Subject refers to any individual who uses our Service and is the subject of Personal Information.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may collect the following information:

  • Workout data (timer settings, sets, reps, rest intervals)
  • Exercise history (completed workout sessions and duration)
  • Health data (workout data synced with Apple HealthKit, with your permission)
  • Purchase information (subscription status managed through RevenueCat)
  • Usage data (e.g., how you interact with the app)

We only collect and use this information to:

  • Provide workout tracking and timer functionality
  • Record workout data to Apple HealthKit (optional)
  • Manage premium subscriptions
  • Improve the Service

2. HealthKit Integration

BulkUp integrates with Apple HealthKit to record your workout data. This integration is optional and requires your explicit permission.

  • We only write workout data to HealthKit; we do not read any health data from your device
  • HealthKit data follows Apple's strict privacy guidelines
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in your device settings

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Workout data is stored locally using SwiftData
  • HealthKit data is managed by Apple's secure framework
  • Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat
  • We do not operate any servers that store your personal information

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your Personal Information based on one of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you provide consent to collect and process your data.
  • Contract: To fulfill a contract with you, such as providing access to our Service.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws.
  • Legitimate interests: For improving our Service, except where overridden by your data protection rights.

5. User Rights

If you are a resident of the EEA or California (under GDPR or CCPA), you have the following rights regarding your Personal Information:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the Personal Information we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to deletion: You can request the deletion of your Personal Information by deleting data within the app or uninstalling the app.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Right to opt-out (CCPA): We do not sell your Personal Information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at pepperfactory.dev@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services that may collect and process your data. These services include:

  • Apple HealthKit - for recording workout data (optional)
  • RevenueCat - for subscription management
  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements (free version)
  • Firebase Analytics - for anonymized analytics

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

7. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by clearing data in the app or uninstalling the app.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including storing data using Apple's secure frameworks. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users through the app where appropriate.

This policy is effective as of 2024-12-28.
