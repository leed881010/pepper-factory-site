Privacy Policy

Pepper Factory Apps

Last updated: December 28, 2024

Deokhwa Lee ("we," "us," or "our") operates the following mobile applications (collectively, the "Services"): OneThing, Habit, BulkUp, and AdMob Tracker.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our Services, as well as your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

The information we collect varies depending on which app you use:

OneThing (Pomodoro Timer)

  • Timer records (focus time, break time, session counts)
  • To-do lists and task content
  • D-Day events (dates and titles)
  • App usage statistics (anonymized)

Habit (Habit Tracker)

  • Habit data (names, icons, colors, target days)
  • Completion records and history
  • Reminder settings
  • App usage statistics (anonymized)

BulkUp (Workout Timer)

  • Workout data (timer settings, sets, reps, rest intervals)
  • Exercise history and duration
  • Health data synced with Apple HealthKit (with your permission)
  • Subscription status
  • App usage statistics (anonymized)

AdMob Tracker

  • Google Account information (for authentication)
  • AdMob revenue data (accessed via Google AdMob API)
  • App usage statistics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Services
  • To sync your data across devices (via iCloud where applicable)
  • To send notifications and reminders you've configured
  • To process subscriptions and payments
  • To improve our Services through anonymized analytics

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device and, where applicable, in your personal iCloud account. We do not store any personal information on our servers.

  • Local data is stored using Apple's secure frameworks (SwiftData)
  • iCloud synchronization follows Apple's security policies
  • Authentication tokens (AdMob Tracker) are stored in iOS Keychain
  • HealthKit data (BulkUp) is managed by Apple's secure framework

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud - Data synchronization (OneThing, Habit)
  • Apple HealthKit - Workout data recording (BulkUp)
  • Google Sign-In & AdMob API - Authentication and data access (AdMob Tracker)
  • Google AdMob - Advertisements (free versions)
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management (BulkUp)
  • Amplitude - Anonymized analytics

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are a resident of the EEA or California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to deletion: Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Revoke permissions at any time
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell your personal information

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
